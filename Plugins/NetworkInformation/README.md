here's the settings
### Main Settings
`Show NetworkOwned Objects V1 (local):` shows part that are "network owned" (note V1 actually heights parts that "physically simulated" or whatever that means but it's close to NetworkShip. also the reason why theres a (local) it's cuz it only highlights parts that are effected by you and not other players

`Show NetworkOwned Objects V2 (global):` almost same as V1 but it actually shows NetworkOwned part accurately but it's global it also highlights parts that are effected by other players. if you find this annoying just use V1

`Show All SimulationRadiuses:` shows players (including you) SimulationRadiuse. that's it

`Show UnAchored Parts:` it highlights parts that **aren't anchored** (that doesn't always mean you have net ownership over them it just highlights unachored parts)

### Miscellaneous
`Animations Info:` basic animation info from your humanoid

`Raknet Desync:` just `raknet.desync` you can learn about raknet.desync just sreach it up. if your executor doesn't support it this setting won't be shown in the UI

`Cap Ping at 999+:` at the bottom of the UI there wull be a ping counter. enabling Cap Ping will make it so the ping will only show 999+ if it surpasses 999
